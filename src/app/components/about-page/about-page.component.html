Author and Creator
Lisa Hildebrand
Lisa is a wife and mother of two who lives in Ludlow Falls, Ohio. She graduated from Slippery Rock University with a BA in both special Education and Elementary Education and has spent the last 26 years working in the area of special education. Lisa is a lover of all things for children. When she’s not at work, she can be found kayaking, reading a book, or riding her bike. She also spends countless hours tryingt to convince friends and family to try something new with her, such as learning to play the ukulele, sleeping in a floating tent, eatign at a new restaurant, or cardio drumming! Lisa enjoys organizing events and volunteering whenever possible at church and community events

Parker was born with colorblindness and has foudn that he enjoys the challenges that come along with bringing these imates to full color.
