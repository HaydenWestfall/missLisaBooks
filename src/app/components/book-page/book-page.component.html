Fenner Road Series
Madison and her little brother live in the country they are surrounded by wildlife. Early one summer day, Madison and her mom discover a snapping turtle laying eggs. Madison is determined to save the turtle eggs from being eaten by local wildlife, but its not an easy job.

Is she up to the task?

Based on a true story, follow Madison's adventure in Hatching Snappers, a heartwarming tale of determination and nature.
